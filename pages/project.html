Robot Hunt | 2015
  • iOS
  • Objective C
  • Cloud Foundry
  • Node JS
  • iBeacons

The mission with 'Robot Hunt' was to create an immersive mobile app activity built on IBM's Cloud to showcase at some of IBM's prime events: Pulse, and InterConnect. Participants would use the app to detect giant robot zip banners at a huge indoor expo. They walked around and watched the needle on a guage twitch if they approached a robot. A prize for finding all six robots.

The system used iBeacons for indoor location. I wanted to hint at the possible real world use cases for this, and was surprised at the ideas that came back from customers. From promotions at retail stores, to guidance on crusie ships, and check in at gym equipment.

It was about six weeks of work to design, experiment with iBeacons, build the app and publish on the app store. This was my first published app. Back then it took about two weeks for approval.
